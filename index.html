Tommaso Cattani - Game/Level Designer

Hey There! I'm Tommaso Cattani, and I usually sign my work as "Tom Curly".
I'm a 22 years old Junior Game Designer and I just graduated from TheSign Comics & Arts Academy with full marks. I love making personal interactive experiences, that feel good to play and also talk about deep, intimate themes.
I'm interested in any kind of creative experience, so feel free to contact me!

Skills

  • Game Design

    I'm confident in designing and fleshing out unique, interesting Video Game concepts, while also knowing when to cut ideas to achieve an elegant design and systems. I'm also knowledgeable in User Experience and Game Feel.

  • Level Design

    I'm able to design and build levels that reinforce the game's themes, while elegantly teaching players how to approach and grow in the experience. I also have much experience with building my and other designer's levels in-engine.

  • Unity Engine

    I'm well-versed with the Unity Engine, with a good grasp on its many features, mostly in order to prototype and build levels. I also have experience with GameMaker Studio and Unreal Engine 4, but my specialty lies with Unity.

  • Digital Art

    I'm capable of creating different kinds of digital art, hand-drawn or 3D, but Pixel Art and animation is my favourite and most experienced form of art.

  • C# Programming

    I'm able to quickly code unoptimized but functional C# code for the Unity Engine, in order to translate ideas to playable prototypes as soon as possible.
