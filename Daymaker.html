Overview

Daymaker with Tom Curly is a Puzzle Platformer with meta elements, in which the player has to modify files on their desktop in order to explore a weird, ruined world. The game's main emotional hooks are alienation and loneliness.

Progress

Currently a simple Prototype that explores the game's main loop: Explore on a Time limit, Get kicked out of the game, modify text files and then re-open the game, now with new abilities and/or level changes.
