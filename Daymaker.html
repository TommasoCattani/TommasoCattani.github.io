Overview

Daymaker with Tom Curly is a Puzzle Platformer with meta
elements, in which the player has to modify files on their
desktop in order to explore a weird, ruined world.

Progress

Currently a simple Prototype that explores the game's main loop:
Explore on a Time limit, Get kicked out of the game,
modify text files and then re-open the game, now with new abilities.

Inspired By:

Petscop, Oneshot, ENA Animated Shorts.
