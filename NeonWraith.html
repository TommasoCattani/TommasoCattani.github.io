Overview

NeonWraith is a 2D top-down Hack 'n' Slash in which you fight your way through floors of Cyborg Rats, with the power of coming back to life as a vengeful Wraith after being killed.
The game's theme and focus is Revenge, and how it affects us.


Progress

Currently a "Slice of Cake" Prototype: the game's core loop is present: combat and dialogue are both functional.
I want to keep working on the game to fully flesh it out and explore a revenge-themed narrative.


Inspirations

Katana Zero, Hotline Miami, Samurai movies.
