Overview

NeonWraith is a 2D top-down Hack 'n' Slash in which you
fight your way through floors of Cyborg Rats, with the
power of coming back to life as a vengeful Wraith after being killed.
The game's main theme is Revenge.


Progress

Currently a "Slice of Cake" Prototype: the game's core loop
is present: combat and dialogue are both functional.
I want to keep working on the game to fully flesh it
out and explore a revenge-themed narrative.


Inspirations

Katana Zero, Hotline Miami, Samurai movies.
